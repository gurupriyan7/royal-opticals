Why Choose Royal Eye Clinic and Opticals

At Royal Eye Clinic and Opticals, we believe your eyes deserve nothing but the best. With over 15 years of dedicated service since our establishment in 2009, we have built a reputation for excellence in eye care. Our clinic is led by Dr. M Chummar Augustus, a highly experienced and qualified eye specialist committed to providing accurate diagnosis and compassionate care.

We use state-of-the-art computerised eye testing to ensure precise results and personalized vision solutions. Whether you’re looking for a regular eye check-up or the latest in premium eyewear, we offer a seamless experience from consultation to selection. With a wide range of global brands and the option for frame and lens customization, we make sure your style and comfort are never compromised. Your vision is our priority — and your satisfaction is our promise.
