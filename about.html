About Royal Eye Clinic and Opticals

Established in 2009, Royal Eye Clinic and Opticals has been a trusted name in quality eye care and premium eyewear in Cherthala. Located just south of the Government Hospital, we are committed to providing personalized and professional eye health services for all age groups. Led by Dr. M Chummar Augustus (M.B.B.S, M.S. (Oph), D.O), a highly experienced eye specialist and registered civil surgeon, our clinic offers accurate diagnoses and effective treatments with compassion and care.

We specialize in computerised eye testing, prescription lenses, and a wide selection of 50+ global eyewear brands, including Ray-Ban, Gucci, Oakley, Titan, Essilor, Zeiss, and more. With a focus on clarity, comfort, and customization, we ensure each customer leaves with perfect vision and a confident look.
